POPOPINION (Django Website)


POPOPINION is a social site where you can share your own thoughts and opinions honestly, about ongoing social issues without the fear of being judged . You can post your opinion via Confessions and have a rational and healthy discussion about it with others in comments section. You can easily ask a question via Polls and see the results instantly to know how many actually agree with you. Popopinion Polls allow you to vote in on questions posted by other people as well.
